中国 · 蜜月之旅
A three-week loop through southern China: pandas in Chengdu, the vertical streets of Chongqing, tropical beaches in Sanya, Cantonese Guangzhou, futuristic Shenzhen, and the old harbour cities of Hong Kong and Macau.

28 June – 18 July 2026. A honeymoon built around modern China at full tilt, bullet trains and mountain metros, panda forests and tropical reefs, teahouses and night markets, with the deep history underneath it all. We begin inland in Chengdu, climb through the vertical city of Chongqing, slow down on the beaches of Hainan, then trace the Pearl River Delta through Cantonese Guangzhou and futuristic Shenzhen before finishing where East met West: Hong Kong and Macau.
